# Cleanup Summary

## Overview
Performed bookkeeping cleanup for a small service business covering January 2025.

---

## Key Issues Identified
- Duplicate income entries overstating revenue
- Personal expenses recorded as business expenses
- Uncategorized and unclear transactions
- Improper classification of transfers

---

## Actions Performed
- Removed duplicate transactions
- Reclassified personal expenses to Owner’s Draw
- Categorized all expenses into appropriate accounts
- Reviewed and corrected transaction classifications

---

## Results
- Financial records are now accurate and reliable
- Revenue and expenses properly stated
- Accounts ready for reconciliation and reporting

---

## Recommendation
- Perform monthly bookkeeping to maintain accuracy
- Separate personal and business accounts
- Implement consistent expense categorization
